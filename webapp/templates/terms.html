Socials Auto-Publisher ("the App") is a personal, single-operator automation tool built and operated by Mykhailo Kolomoitsev ("the Operator"). It is not offered as a public service or made available for sign-up by third parties.

1. What the App does

The App lets the Operator send a video to a private Telegram bot. The App then automatically: removes silent segments from the video, generates subtitles via a speech-to-text service, burns the subtitles into the video, generates a cover image, stores the resulting files in cloud storage, and publishes the video to the Operator's own TikTok account. The following day, the App checks the view counts of videos published the prior day via the TikTok API and republishes only the best-performing video to the Operator's own Instagram account as a Reel.

2. Who can use the App

Access to the Telegram bot is restricted to a single, hardcoded Telegram user ID belonging to the Operator. No other person can submit content to the App or trigger publishing through it.

3. Accounts and authorization

The App connects to TikTok and Instagram using OAuth credentials issued directly to the Operator's own accounts on those platforms. The App does not request or store credentials for any other person's account.
